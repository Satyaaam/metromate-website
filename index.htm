FAQ

How do I use Metromate to find a metro route?

Metromate makes it easy to find a metro route. Simply enter your starting point and destination in the provided form, and click "Find Route." We'll display the best route for you.

Is Metromate available on mobile devices?

Yes, Metromate is accessible on all devices, including mobile phones and tablets. You can use it on the go to plan your metro routes.

How accurate are the route calculations?

Metromate provides highly accurate metro route calculations. Our C++ backend ensures precision and reliability in finding the best routes for you.

Do I need to create an account to use Metromate?

No, you can use Metromate without creating an account. It's a hassle-free experience that doesn't require any sign-up.

Are there any additional features for premium users?

Yes, premium users enjoy priority access to real-time updates, ad-free experience, and the ability to save their favorite routes for quick access.
